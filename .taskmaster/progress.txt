- Learning: Reassigning a typed `Array[Vector2i]` field (e.g. `player.segments = [Vector2i(...), ...]`) with an untyped array literal raises a runtime "Invalid assignment of property" and silently aborts the rest of the awaiting function — but `_ready` still resumes after `await` and prints OK on empty failures, so the test looked green while skipping cases.
